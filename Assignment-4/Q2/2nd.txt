Q2)what are html entities? list out 5 commonly used html entities?

Ans= Html entities are reserved character that are used in  the html document.
they are not present in our standard keyboard. html entities provide wide range of characters.
such like add icons, geometric shapes, mathematical operators and so on.

list 5 commonly used html entities below =
1)less than (&lt;)
2)greater  than (&gt;)
3)ampersand  (&amp;)
4)double quotation mark (&quot;)
5)non breking space (&nbsp;)
